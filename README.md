# Math Intellect

## AI-Powered Mathematics Learning and Assessment Platform



#### Founder: Ngwe Htoon

#### Role: Mathematics Educator · AI Workflow Designer · EdTech Product Builder



\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*

###### \* Project Overview \*

\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*



Math Intellect is an AI-assisted learning platform designed to support mathematics education in international schools. The project combines mathematics pedagogy, workflow automation, and large language models (LLM) to transform handwritten examination questions into structured, examiner-style solutions.



Built on n8n, LINE Messaging API, and OpenAI, the platform allows students and teachers to submit mathematics problems through a familiar messaging interface and receive step-by-step solutions, assessment insights, and marking guidance in real time.



The current prototype focuses on Cambridge IGCSE Mathematics and reflects both classroom teaching practice and examination standards.



\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*

###### \* The Problem \*

\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*



International school teachers spend a significant amount of time preparing worksheets, producing mark schemes, and providing individual feedback. Students, meanwhile, often receive answers without understanding the reasoning process behind them. And they can't ask the teachers 24/7.



Existing AI chatbots generate mathematical solutions, but they rarely follow examination requirements or provide outputs aligned with standard curriculum objectives.



Math Intellect was created to address these challenges:



&#x09;Reduce the workload involved in mathematics assessment.

&#x09;Deliver transparent, step-by-step mathematical reasoning with proper mark scheme.

&#x09;Bridge the gap between AI-generated answers and classroom practice.

&#x09;Assist anytime and anywhere with internet access.

###### \* Core Features \*

\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*



AI-Powered Question Recognition



&#x09;Receives handwritten or printed mathematics questions through LINE.

&#x09;Extracts mathematical content directly from uploaded images.

&#x09;Supports examination-style question formats.



Multi-Layer Reasoning Engine



The platform does not rely on a single AI response. Instead, it employs a structured verification pipeline:



&#x09;Question Analyzer

&#x20;       	↓

&#x09;      Solver

&#x20;       	↓

&#x09;     Examiner

&#x20;       	↓

&#x09;    Moderator

&#x20;       	↓

&#x09;Quality Checker

&#x20;       	↓

&#x09;    Formatter



Each layer performs an independent role to improve consistency, explainability, and output quality.



Examination-Oriented Output



&#x09;Step-by-step mathematical solutions.

&#x09;Cambridge-style marking logic.

&#x09;Assessment objective classification.

&#x09;Structured feedback suitable for both students and teachers.

###### \* Long-Term Vision \*

\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*





Math Intellect aims to build an AI-native educational infrastructure that enhances mathematics learning while preserving the role of teachers and let students to adaptive easy way of learning with smart devices at anytime and anywhere.





The objective is not to replace educators, but to augment teaching practice through intelligent automation, explainable reasoning, and scalable educational technology.
