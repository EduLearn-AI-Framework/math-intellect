Educational Pain Points

\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*



For Teachers

* &#x09;Producing worksheets and mark schemes is time-consuming.
* &#x09;Individual feedback becomes difficult as class sizes increase.
* &#x09;Existing AI tools generate answers that do not align with examination expectations.
* &#x09;Teachers need explainable solutions, not only final answers.



For Students

* &#x09;Students often receive solutions without understanding the reasoning behind them.
* &#x09;Many AI systems skip intermediate steps without mark scheme.
* &#x20;    AI can give only solution, but may be wrong and not aligned with Cambridge IGCSE syllabus.
* &#x09;It is difficult to identify mistakes and misconceptions.
* &#x09;Access to quality mathematics support outside school hours remains limited.
